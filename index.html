GLOBAL REFORESTATION PROJECT

One Child, One Fruit Tree, One Eternal Legacy.

"Our mission is simple yet absolute: to provide every student in Bangladesh, from Class 1 to 10, with their own fruit-bearing tree. In an era of environmental decline, we believe in planting the seeds of both nutrition and nature. Many children in underprivileged communities lack access to essential vitamins; we provide the source.

100% of your contribution is used strictly for purchasing trees. We bear all logistical, travel, and operational costs personally. Your donation plants hope."

Why Fruit Trees?

Traditional reforestation often ignores immediate human needs. By planting fruit trees, we provide children with a sustainable source of nutrition while restoring the ecosystem. It is a dual-purpose investment in the planet and the next generation.

Where Does Your Money Go?

Transparency is our foundation. We have invested zero donor capital into this website or our operations. Every dollar you send is converted directly into a sapling. Our team covers all personal expenses, transportation, and labor costs out of pocket.

Global Scalability

We begin in Bangladesh, a nation facing severe climate vulnerability. Once our mission of 1 Billion trees is achieved here, we will expand our reach to other nations in need of ecological balance and food security.

The Silent Witness

Every tree is recorded in the ARK ARCHIVE. By claiming a plot, your name is etched into the digital record of this revolution—a testament to your contribution to a cleaner, greener, and healthier world.
