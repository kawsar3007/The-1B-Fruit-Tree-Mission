"Our mission is simple yet absolute: to provide every student in Bangladesh, from Class 1 to 10, with their own fruit-bearing tree. In an era of environmental decline, we believe in planting the seeds of both nutrition and nature. 100% of your contribution is used strictly for purchasing trees. We bear all logistical, travel, and operational costs personally. Your donation plants hope."

Why Fruit Trees?

By planting fruit trees, we provide children with a sustainable source of nutrition while restoring the ecosystem.

Zero Cost Operations

Every dollar you send is converted directly into a sapling. Our team covers all labor and transportation out of pocket.

Global Scalability

We begin in Bangladesh, but we are starting for all nations. Our vision is for 1 Billion trees globally.
