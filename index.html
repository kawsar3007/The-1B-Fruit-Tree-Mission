OUR GLOBAL COMMITMENT

Global Scalability

Starting in Bangladesh, our mission is to provide every student (Class 1-10) with a fruit-bearing tree. We are now expanding this vision to students in every climate-vulnerable nation across the globe.

Financial Integrity

We invest ZERO donor capital into website maintenance, marketing, or salaries. 100% of your funds are strictly used for purchasing saplings. All operational costs are personally funded by our team.

Why Fruit Trees?

By planting fruit trees, we address two crises at once: environmental decline and childhood malnutrition. It is a dual-purpose investment in the planet and the next generation's health.
